Global Sales by Genre

One large way games differ is on their genre. A player won't go into a shooter game expecting the same experience as a puzzle game, so each game's financial success may vary between the constraints and/or allowances of the particular genre is falls into.

Here in this barplot, we can see the trends in what video game genres tend to sell the most.
